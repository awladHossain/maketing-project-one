The field of preparing strategic plans

This field aims to assist the beneficiaries in building their strategic plans in accordance with the scientific foundations. This field includes assisting the beneficiaries in diagnosing their current reality, including analyzing the internal and external work environment, employing the best tools for analysis and comparison, and then identifying and analyzing the gap, and accurately defining the vision and mission, and strategic goals and values, and identifying the most important indicators and resources needed for success, in order to help the beneficiaries to raise the efficiency of their performance

02

The field of quality studies

This field focuses on helping the beneficiaries to apply quality methodologies, in order to achieve effective control of the various processes and procedures and the optimal investment of various material and human resources and thus improve the performance of the entity

03

The field of institutional performance evaluation studies

This field aims to focus on evaluating the institutional performance of the beneficiaries, by focusing on the activities and actions of the beneficiary in light of its interaction with the elements of the internal and external environment surrounding it, and the extent of its use and optimization of the various available financial, human, organizational and technical resources, in order to achieve an optimal investment of these elements and resources, and thus identifying the most important strengths and weaknesses in that performance, in order to help these entities achieve an effective level of performance The Marketing Incubator seeks to establish the concept of quality, strategy, innovation and excellence, in order to raise institutional performance, increase productivity and create a supportive environment, through
